Love Anonymous

Love Anonymous is an archival project that collects and shares anonymous messages from people in New York City. After gathering these handwritten notes, I created a digital platform that preserves them as part of a growing online archive. Each message is written on a playing card, transforming a familiar and randomized object into something deeply personal. Each card is unique yet part of a larger system, the project highlights the diversity and individuality of these anonymous voices.

To present the archive, I designed and developed a website that functions like a digital museum experience. Visitors can scroll through the cards as if moving through a deck; when hovered, each card flips to reveal its message, and clicking on it allows viewers to explore it more closely. Alongside the website, I created an Instagram page to expand the reach of the project and share these messages with a wider audience, turning the archive into an evolving social platform for anonymous expressions.
