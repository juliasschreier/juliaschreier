Combinature

Combinature explores the interaction between two-dimensional drawings and three-dimensional surfaces. My goal was to investigate how repetition influences the effectiveness of a drawing within a three-dimensional space. Through this project, I created a visual representation of the fusion between man-made objects and nature, examining their coexistence and interaction. The composition relies on line work to construct realistic depictions of various elements, bringing them to life within the spatial form.
