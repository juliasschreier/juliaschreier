Rosh Hashanah Holiday Cards

I interned at CBH (Compagnie Bancaire Helvétique) and worked with their communications and marketing team on designing a series of Rosh Hashanah cards. The cards were thoughtfully crafted to reflect the spirit of the holiday, incorporating traditional themes and symbols associated with Rosh Hashanah. I ensured brand consistency by working within the company’s established color palette. Using Adobe Illustrator, I created visually engaging and meaningful designs that conveyed warm holiday wishes while aligning with the bank’s brand identity.
