Lagé: Museum of Collage

This project focused on promotion, packaging, and design for a conceptual collage museum opening. I developed a cohesive visual identity by creating promotional materials, including posters, business cards, and potential packaging designs for the museum’s gift shop. Inspired by the art of collage, I explored how to balance a bold and visually dynamic theme while maintaining effective communication with the target audience.

Through careful consideration of typography, color schemes, and layout, I ensured that the designs captured the museum’s creative spirit while remaining functional as promotional materials. This project allowed me to experiment with combining chaotic, layered visuals with clear, engaging messaging.
